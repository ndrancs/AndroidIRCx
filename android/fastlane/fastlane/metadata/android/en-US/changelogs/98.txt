New WebRTC audio and video calling with Privacy Relay support.
Free users now get direct P2P calls with configurable STUN servers, 480p default and optional 720p, smarter signaling, smaller SDP payloads, retry/fallback handling, and clearer connection errors.
Calls can be minimized with PiP/overlay and ongoing notifications.
Added return/hang up actions, query restore, overlay sizing, and camera/mic permission prompts before calls.
